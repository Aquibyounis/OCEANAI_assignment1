# Product Specifications

## Discount Codes
- **SAVE15**: Applies a 15% discount to the subtotal.
- **OCEAN20**: Applies a 20% discount to the subtotal.

## Shipping Rules
- **Standard Shipping**: Free (0.00 USD). Delivery in 5-7 business days.
- **Express Shipping**: $10.00 USD. Delivery in 1-2 business days.

## Cart Limits
- Maximum quantity per item: 10
- Minimum order value for checkout: None

## Tax
- Sales tax is calculated at 0% for this demo.
